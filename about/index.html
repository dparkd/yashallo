Norway x Yas Hallo

Creating products with a mission

yas hallo is a minimal brand that partners with short-term missionaries to donate 100% of sale profits to support their journey. Every missionary that partners with yas hallo will get their own product designed through a collaboration.

Know a missionary that wants to collaborate with yas hallo? Send us a dm on instagram @ yas_hallo

Crafted by Daniel

Daniel created yas hallo in order to provide for those who are going on missions. Through creative collaboration he believes that yas hallo can make an impact in the world no matter how small it may be.
